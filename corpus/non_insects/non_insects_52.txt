      <text bytes="516" sha1="4dmwdsggokrhnoqomzxpkpf2nbla239" xml:space="preserve">[[File:Deer_tick.jpg|thumb|194px]]''Ixodes scapularis'' or the '''Deer tick''' is a [[tick]] found throughout the US and other parts of the world. A female deer tick will drink blood for 4 or 5 days, and then will drop off of the host, and then lays her eggs. A deer tick drinks blood to reproduce, but during the drinking of blood, the tick could transmit diseases. The order of this tick is [[Acari]] and the family is [[Ixodidae]].

      <text bytes="486" sha1="1s61dirapl3j6kj70lnkcbauu6anv1j" xml:space="preserve">[[File:Deer_tick.jpg|thumb|168px|The [[Deer Tick]]]]A tick is an [[arachnid]] that lives in long grass, bushes, and trees. All ticks are small and could be hard or soft depending on the species and genus. All ticks belong to the [[Acari]] order and most belong to the [[Ixodidae]] or [[Argasidae]] families. Ticks mainly take blood from humans and animals. These arachnids can transmit disease to you if you have been bitten.

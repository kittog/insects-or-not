A puss moth is a [[Lepidoptera|lepidopteran]] from the family [[Notodontidae]]. The species was first described by Carl Linnaeus in his 1758 10th edition of Systema Naturae.&lt;br&gt;

*Scientific name: [[Cerura vinula]]&lt;br&gt;

*Order: [[Lepidoptera]]&lt;br&gt;

*Family: [[Notodontidae]]&lt;br&gt;

*Phylum: [[Arthropoda]]&lt;br&gt;

*Kingdom: [[Animalia]]&lt;br&gt;

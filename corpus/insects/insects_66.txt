      <text bytes="590" sha1="j540tayro83jljvup7aikeuohsiquu4" xml:space="preserve">[[File:Blastophaga_psenes.jpg|thumb|218px]]''Blastophaga psenes'' is a species of [[fig wasp]] that is found all around the world. It commonly pollinates ''Ficus carica'', the common fig. There are 10 times more females of this species than there are males. The males of this species do not have wings.



Loboptera decipiens
[[File:Loboptera decipiens.jpg|thumb]]''Loboptera decipiens'' is a species of [[cockroach]] that belongs to the [[Blattelidae]] family, and the [[genus]] ''[[Loboptera]]''. 
This species is wingless, and native to Europe.
